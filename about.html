Our History

In line with our mission and vision of raising Learners who are thoroughly furnished spiritually, mentally, morally and socially, climax school was established on the 19th of September, 2011.
It's name was later changed to Elixir Nursery and Primary School on the 13th October, 2017 after registering with the Lagos State Ministry of Education.

We operate a comprehensive curriculum as approved by the Ministry of Education. Since inception, Elixir School has imbibed in her Learners the culture of excellence and godliness. As our motto is " Excellence and Godliness".
